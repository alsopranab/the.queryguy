# NETWORKDAYS & WORKDAY

## What they do
Calculate working days excluding weekends (and holidays).

## Syntax
`=NETWORKDAYS(start_date, end_date, [holidays])`  
`=WORKDAY(start_date, days, [holidays])`

## Example
`=NETWORKDAYS(A2, B2)`  
`=WORKDAY(A2, 10)`

## Business Use
Project timelines and SLA tracking.

## Interview Tip
Mention holidays parameter for real-world usage.
