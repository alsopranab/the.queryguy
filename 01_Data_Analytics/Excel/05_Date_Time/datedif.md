# DATEDIF

## What it does
Calculates difference between two dates.

## Syntax
`=DATEDIF(start_date, end_date, unit)`

## Units
- `"Y"` → years  
- `"M"` → months  
- `"D"` → days  

## Example
`=DATEDIF(A2, B2, "D")`

## Business Use
Tenure calculation, age calculation.

## Interview Note
DATEDIF is undocumented but heavily used.
